Privacy Policy for TapMiles Mileage Tracker

Effective date: February 21, 2026

TapMiles Mileage Tracker does not collect, store, or share personal data.

This app is designed to work locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, tracking data, or location data on our servers.

2. How the App Works

TapMiles Mileage Tracker is a mileage logging app that helps users track trips and mileage. Data entered into the app is stored locally on the user’s device unless the user chooses to export it using features provided by the device or operating system.

3. Location Data

If the app uses location services on your device to help calculate trip mileage, that data is processed on-device and is not collected by us, unless explicitly stated in a future version of the app.

4. Third-Party Services

TapMiles Mileage Tracker does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services that collect personal data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data because we do not collect it.

6. Data Retention

Because we do not collect data on our servers, we do not retain personal data.

7. Children’s Privacy

TapMiles Mileage Tracker does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
